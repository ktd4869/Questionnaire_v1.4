Task Tutorial

  1. Click to expand each task one by one to answer questions or label articles.
  2. In Task 2 or 4, read the article, select the technical word/phrase (phrase: 5 words maximum), and select its difficulty level (1~10) in the prompt (the word/phrase will be highlighted in selected color).
  3. Right click your highlighted words(s) to delete an annotation.
  4. When you select the phrase which contains highlighted words, you need to remove (right click) the highlighted words first.
  5. Click "Save Task" button to save your answers (the button will turn to be green).
  6. Click to collapse the completed task.
  7. Submit after completing all five tasks.

Difficulty Level Standard

  • 1~2 Very easy 
    • eg, email, mobile
  •  3~4 Easy
    • eg, compromise, file heritage
  • 5~6 Normal
    • eg, defender
  • 7~8 Hard
    • eg, evade
  • 9~10 Challenging
    • eg, Distributed Denial of Service attack
